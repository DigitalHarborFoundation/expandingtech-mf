Setting Up Gmail On Your Device

Setting Up Gmail on Your iPhone or iPad Using the Gmail or Inbox

It’s very simple to set up Google’s Gmail to sync to your iPhone or iPad. This is especially convenient on occasions when you don’t have access to a laptop. Here’s how:

    • Open the App Store

    • Tap on Mail
    • Tap Search in the bottom-right part of the screen. Type Gmail into the search field.
    • Choose either Gmail or Inbox by Gmail – both are official Google apps that allow you to send and receive Gmail messages on your iPhone. If you’re interested in setting up non-Gmail accounts as well, the Inbox app will allow you to do so.

    • Tap Get to the right of the app. When the button label changes to Install, tap it again and the icon will be added to your home screen.
    • You can now tap Open in the location where the Get and Install buttons had been.
    • When a window appears saying “Gmail Would Like to Send You Notifications”, you can tap allow to let the app send you push notifications when you receive mail.

  • Tap Sign In at the bottom of the screen. Add your Gmail account by tapping + Add Account at the bottom of the list and entering your Gmail address and password.
  • Tap Done in the upper left corner of the screen

Setting Up Gmail On Your Android

    • Open the App Drawer by swiping up from the dock or tapping the App Drawer icon

    • Tap Settings

    • Go to Accounts > Add Account, and then select Google

    • Enter your password, fingerprint, or pattern and enter your DHF Gmail address at the Add Your Account screen. Tap Next.

    • Select Agree on the Terms of Service page. You can skip adding payment information by selecting No Thanks
    • Once finished, you will return to the Accounts in Settings. You can view accounts you’ve added or delete any by tapping Google
